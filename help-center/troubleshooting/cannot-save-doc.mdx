---
title: Unable to Save Documentation
description: Quick fix when changes don't save.
---

## Symptoms

- Clicking "Save" does nothing
- Error: "Saving failed"

## Possible Causes

- Lost internet connection
- Session expired

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Connection">
    ````bash
    Visit another site or refresh Paradoha gh to test your connection.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Login Again">
    Log out, then log in again. Try saving your doc.
  </Step>
</Steps>
